Hike Up The Mountain

May 27, 2016 / Brian Lusina

Hiking. Not Mathew's favorite activity. In fact, if he had it any other way, he would be in the valley lying on his back on a picnic mat as his girlfriend told him of her long week and her female workmates who always seemed to be scheming for one reason or the other. "I was never good news, I wonder why she can't just get another job then", Mathew thought to himself as he fastened the heavy backpack on his back. They hike was going to be long and treacherous, but he was prepared, mentally at least. Physically, he would just have to sweat out the pain. They said this mountain had never been conquered, Mathew was not even going to test that theory, he was just doing this to get his girlfriend of his back about not being adventuruous. He decided this had to be done to at least to make her stop nagging him constantly. So, he picked Mt. Susa. The tallest in his country. This was surely going to send a statement. But, when they got to the foot of the mountain, he quickly wondered whether he could have probably taken a second longer just to think about it
