The Wise Man And The Boy

May 31, 2016 / Brian Lusina

He was quite the nuisance in the community. Wrecking havoc everywhere he went. His parents knew that, but they did not know what to do. "He is like the devil! I told you to be careful with this one." Harim told his brother. Harim was never known for withholding his thoughts, even though they seemed cruel and mean. He always said what he felt needed to be said. After all, the truth shall set you free. "He is not like the devil, he is a cheeky small boy, still trying to find himself," the father tried to defend the small boy even though deep down he knew that he did not fully believe his own words. Regardless, he was his son and he had a responsibility of making sure he grew up to be a strong independent man. A man who was supposed to be better than him. But this destructive path was not helping his cause. He was not only embarassing him but also his family's name. Suddenly, a loud bang was heard outside BANG! The two old men jumped up to their feet. "I hope that is not what I think it is!" Harim said as he rushed outside. Musa, the brother, quickly followed him outside.
