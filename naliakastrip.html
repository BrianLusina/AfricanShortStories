Naliaka's Trip

May 29, 2016 / Brian Lusina

Naliaka was not too fond of travelling. She used to love it as a child, but not anymore, she wished she could teleport. One second you're here and the next you're half way across the world. That would be simply fantastic, she could see her parents who had been nagging her to go home to visit and then see her brother who has a new born son and then be able to make her trip to China all in one day. She always wanted to go to China. The culture simply fascinated her and she wanted to have a chance to experience it on her own. She saw the teleportation ability in a movie once, "he just looked at a photo of Paris and then poof he was there!" She told her good friend Najoli who she met with in town at a cafe during lunch hour. "I think I have seen that movie before. It must be nice to be at 10 places in a day and not break a sweat! However, you lose the chance to enjoy the scenery along the way to your destination." Najoli replied as she sipped her coffee, she still never quite understood the fascination with coffee these days.


"Besides, the journey is always better than reaching the destination in under a second." Najolu continued as she swallowed that dark substance. She almost teared a little. "I am just always busy with work, being able to meet all these social obligations in a day, sounds like a major win." Naliaka was never known to be as optimistic as Najoli, she was always cautiously optimistic, it helped her maintain a sort of clarity and keep in touch with reality
