The White Fox

June 3, 2016 / Brian Lusina

Life is always full of surprises and the white fox is not unfamilar to this. He has always been a sly one, the smartest of his pack. The quickest and also witty. However, despite all his abilities, he could not outdo his latest challenge. Despite this, the white fox always loved a challenge. I mean, what is life but a series of challenges thrown at you? The fox knew this, so did the world. However, he was not going to let that stop him. Of course he would have to figure out how to get rid of the coat that looked incriminating. He surely did not do it. How could he? He loved the man. He simply wanted to ask for his blessing for her daughter's hand in marriage. "I swear! I found him like this when I got here." He thought of what to say if he was caught there by the village. But, have those words ever sounded innocent? He was not going to be exiled. Not today at least. So, he hatched a plan...

King Of The Jungle

June 2, 2016 / Brian Lusina

Kings. We do not have many of them in these present times, but we can still feel their prescence from days of old. Well, technically having a president ruling a country can somewhat be almost similar to having a king. The only difference is that they (presidents) can be voted in and out of office, kings on the other hand, can only be removed from power when they lose in battle, or when they die and their heirs take over. Hmm... Actually, the people have no say in the ruling of kings. They are autocratical, ruthless, vicious. Some of them at least. But, not all of them are. They like to think of themselves as gods. They really aren't. They know it, but they want the people to never know it. After all the ruled have no say in matters regarding the rulers. But, is this always the case?...
